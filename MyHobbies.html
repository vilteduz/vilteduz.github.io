My Hobbies

First Hobby

One of my biggest hobbies is dancing. It brings me happiness, and helps when I feel upset, I just get lost in my emotions and dance moves and just let my body flow. I forget all of the worries, things and people that are around me. Dancing has helped me through the hardest moments of my life and I will be forever grateful for that

Second Hobby

My second hobby is horse riding. I don't think it is a very common thing that people like, but for me it is one of the best thigs ever. I fell in love with horses after I watched the movie "Racing Stripes". After that all of my life started spinning around horses. My mom putted me in riding classes and I was doing it for 5 years. But then I got an injury when the horse stepped on my foot, so I had to drop riding classes, which was very sad for me. Right now I'm not taking riding classes, but if I had a chance, I would do it in a heart beat
